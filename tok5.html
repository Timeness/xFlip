Project Vision

NovaDrop aims to disrupt the conventional airdrop meta by introducing fair launch mechanics, whitelist-based participation, and utility-oriented token design. Our vision includes:

  • Verifiable testnet participant rewards through on-chain snapshots
  • Multi-wallet registration prevention via Sybil-resistant tools
  • On-chain referral bonuses and gamified claim system
  • DAO-based decisions on future drops and token utility
  • Cross-chain compatibility with LayerZero and Wormhole
