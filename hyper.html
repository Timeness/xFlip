Our MisSion

To steward and expand the Hyperlane network

The Hyperlane Foundation* represents the interests of users and developers who collectively shape the future of the Hyperlane network and ecosystem. We aim for the Hyperlane network to become the first truly open interoperability framework that is created in collaboration with an engaged community of developers.

*The Developer’s Eminence Foundation is d/b/a as “Hyperlane Foundation”
